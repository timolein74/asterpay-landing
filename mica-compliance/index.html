What is MiCA?

The Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation (MiCA) is the EU's comprehensive regulatory framework for crypto-assets. It establishes uniform rules for crypto-asset service providers (CASPs) across the European Union.

Current Status: Preparing for MiCA

AsterPay is actively preparing for full MiCA compliance. We are committed to operating as a regulated entity under EU law.
