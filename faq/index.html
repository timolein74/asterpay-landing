What is x402?

x402 is an open protocol (by Coinbase) that enables autonomous AI agents to make instant stablecoin payments. Instead of API keys and credit cards, AI agents can pay for services directly using USDC.

AsterPay is the European leader in x402 infrastructure, making it easy for developers to accept x402 payments.

Is AsterPay custodial?

No. AsterPay is completely non-custodial:

  • We never hold your private keys
  • We never take custody of your funds
  • Payments go directly to your wallet
  • You maintain full control at all times
